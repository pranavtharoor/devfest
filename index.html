ABOUT
DevFests are large, community-run developer conferences organized around the globe with a focus on community building and learning about various Google technologies. Women Techmakers and Developer Student Clubs Manipal bring to you DevFest 2018.

Join us on the 23rd of September for one day of talks and workshops on latest Google technologies ranging from Actions on Google to Material Design and Flutter.

This year, we have two concurrent tracks and the attendees may choose to attend one of these based on their interests.

The WTM track will kickoff with an introduction to Material Design, UI/UX and then will move on to mobile app development with Flutter.

The Developer Students' Club will walk the participants through developing Actions For Google Assistant alongside covering Firebase
INSTALL FEST
To participate in the DevFest and to make the most of all the workshops we recommend the attendees to come with all requisite softwares pre-installed.
To help with the process we are organizing an install fest on 21st September 2018. The softwares required are:
  • Android Studio
  • Flutter Plugin
  • Adobe XD
  • Sublime Text
Material Design
& UI/UX
Material Design is a system backed by open-source code that helps teams build digital experiences better. This session will focus on UI/UX that is essential for developing interactive and intuitive applications.

Participants will learn
  • Fundamentals of UI/UX
  • Material Design 2.0 guidelines and tools
  • How to customize Material Design to change the look and feel of your UI
  • Expressing brand and style through elements like color, shape, typography, and iconography and more
  • Making a mock-up of the application in Adobe XD
  • Implementing the design in Flutter
Flutter
Flutter is Google's mobile app SDK for crafting high-quality native interfaces on iOS and Android in record time. Flutter works with existing code, is used by developers and organizations around the world, and is free and open source.

Want to get started on building Flutter apps? Attend the Flutter session during DevFest.

Participants will:
  • Learn about fundamentals: UI Widgets, App State, Material Design, User Interaction and Gestures, and Packages
  • Learn how to structure and architect their app and use Flutter's tooling to build apps efficiently
  • Also be able to get hands-on experience with Flutter and build a Flutter app while learning the fundamentals
  • Learn to implement widgets and develop an app given its UI design and functionality
  • Introduce and implement Material design elements into the app
Actions on
Google
Google Assistant is one of Google's biggest beta with AI and it has recently attracted a lot of interest from users and developers alike. Actions on Google is how developers can extend Assistant to provide amazing features in the form of rich conversational experiences.

This DevFest, join us to learn how to build innovative and unique Actions and publish them to Google Assistant.

Participants will learn:
  • DialogFlow (Intent Matching and Entity Extraction)
  • How to build Conversational Experiences with Actions on Google
  • Conversational Design Introduction
  • Difference between GUI and VUI
  • Top 3 Design tips
  • VUI Best practices
  • How to enhance your Action
  • Codelab: Build Actions for the Google Assistant
Firebase &
Web APIs
Firebase is Google's answer to the common problems that developers usually face, such as database management and deployment, while making applications. In our Firebase workshop, we will introduce the participants to building a backend API for Actions On Google. We will also use various other functionalities provided by Firebase such as Authentication, and Data Storage.

By focusing on walking attendees through developing a product that uses Firebase, we aim show them how developers can easily use these out of the box solutions to go from zero to production-ready in no time.

Participants will learn:
  • Fundamentals of Web APIs
  • Basics of databases
  • Serverless Architectures
  • Developing Web Applications with Firebase
  • User Authentication and Authorization
  • Building a API for Actions On Google
